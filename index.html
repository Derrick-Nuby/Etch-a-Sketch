16 x 16

This is a sketch paper you can draw what you like on it

  1. To start click on the slider to choose your canvas boxes then hover the mouse in the white space and then play
  2. The single color button defaults to black color; so by clicking it you draw in black
  3. By clicking random colors you draw in colorful colr such as rgb and rainbow colours
  4. To erase some colors click the eraser button
  5. To clear all the text click clear all
